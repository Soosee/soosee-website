Breath is the bridge which connects life to consciousness, which unites your body to your thoughts. Thich Nhat Hanh

Unwind is a refreshing and a delightful app consisting of deep breathing exercises to help you wake up fresh, improve focus, and sleep better.

Fall Asleep Faster.

  • Feel awesome, everyday. Unwind comes with three breathing exercises specially made to tackle these three parts of your day. Whenever you breathe with Unwind, something special happens.
  • Crafted just for you. As a part of the onboarding process, Unwind logs your bedtime which helps in displaying the perfect breathing exercise for you whenever you launch the app.
  • Escape the madness. With twenty five and more background scenes and ambiances, you can plan an escape to your favorite place whenever you want.
  • Stay motivated. Unwind lets you set daily mindfulness target, and custom reminders to make sure you stay mindful throughout the day. Never feel down again.
  • Validate the results. If you have an Apple Watch, Unwind will automatically show you the change in your heart rate after each breathing session.
  • Reflect. Unwind securely backs up all your session details with any notes or mood you’ve logged. Reflect on your past and get better with just a tap.
